  • What is AccessArt?
    • To enable people with visual impairments to explore and understand artworks independently, we propose a touchscreen-based mobile application which provides object-level verbal descriptions of a painting upon users' touch while using VoiceOver.
    Our application can help people with visual impairments to freely explore and learn various paintings more in detail with object-level descriptions as well as spatial information such as position and size.
    By using toggle buttons of a menu page, you can filter undesirable attributes out in Object Mode.
