  • How to Use
    • 1. Please activate VoiceOver on iPhone or iPad.
     ((Settings > General > Accessibility > VoiceOver)
    2. Select a painting and navigate to its appreciation page.
     (If you want to hear an overall description of the painting, wait for a second after highlighting the image of the painting.)
    3. Explore the painting using touch or swipe gestures.
    4. Use toggle buttons of the menu page to filter undesirable attributes out from Object Mode.
